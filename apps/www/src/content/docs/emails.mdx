# Email Templates

Customize confirmation and welcome emails for your waitlists.

## Overview

0list sends two types of emails when double opt-in is enabled:

1. **Confirmation Email** â€” Sent immediately after signup with a verification link
2. **Welcome Email** â€” Sent after the user confirms their email

Both templates can be customized per-waitlist in the admin dashboard under **Settings** â†’ **Email**.

---

## Confirmation Email

This email is sent when a user signs up and double opt-in is enabled. It contains a unique confirmation link.

### Available Variables

| Variable | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| `{{name}}` | User's name (if provided) |
| `{{email}}` | User's email address |
| `{{position}}` | Current waitlist position |
| `{{confirmUrl}}` | Unique confirmation link **(required)** |
| `{{waitlistName}}` | Name of the waitlist |

<CodeBlock
  title="Default Template"
  lang="html"
  code={`<h1>Confirm your spot on {{waitlistName}}</h1>

<p>Hi {{name}},</p>

<p>Thanks for signing up! Click the button below to confirm your email address and secure your spot.</p>

<a href="{{confirmUrl}}" style="
  display: inline-block;
  padding: 12px 24px;
  background: #f97316;
  color: white;
  text-decoration: none;
  border-radius: 6px;
">
  Confirm Email
</a>

<p>You're currently #{{position}} on the waitlist.</p>

<p>If you didn't sign up, you can ignore this email.</p>`}
/>

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
  The confirmation email must include `{{confirmUrl}}` or users won't be able to verify their email.
</Callout>

## Email Branding

Customize the sender information and visual branding for each waitlist.

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **From Name** | The name shown in the recipient's inbox |
| **From Email** | The email address emails are sent from |
| **Reply-To** | Where replies are directed |
| **Logo URL** | Your company logo (displayed in email header) |
| **Primary Color** | Brand color for buttons and links |
| **Company Name** | Used in email footer |

<CodeBlock
  title="Branding Configuration"
  lang="json"
  code={`{
  "fromName": "Acme Inc",
  "fromEmail": "hello@acme.com",
  "replyTo": "support@acme.com",
  "logoUrl": "https://acme.com/logo.png",
  "primaryColor": "#4F46E5",
  "companyName": "Acme Inc"
}`}
/>
